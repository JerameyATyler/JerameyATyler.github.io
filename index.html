What's this?

Sometimes bad things happen. When they do many of us feel like we can't do anything that can make a difference. We don't see how big of an impact we can make if we just don't give up. A problem can seem huge, but if you get enough people to pay attention and they see how much you care, they are usually willing to help you even a little bit. We wanted to find a way to show people how much of an impact they can have without realizing it.
So we are building a thing. It's not done. What you see now is kinda what you'll see when it's finished, but there will be more and it'll be different. We gave ourselves a difficult challenge, and we believe that if we get enough help we can do it. So we want you to watch us as we try to build our thing.
If we finish building our thing, we'll be proud of what we accomplished. If we fail we'll still be proud of what we've learned. We just want to show others that we aren't going to give up.
Come back later and see how we've changed.

Who are we?

We're a bunch of people. Some of us are students, some of us are teachers, some of us are parents, some of us aren't. One of us had an idea and they thought is was a good one. So they told it to a couple of friends. And a couple more. And a couple more. And many of them thought it was a good idea too and decided that they wanted to help. We are all helpers. We are trying to make that one idea come to life. As we get further along we'll update. We'll probably change a lot in a short period of time but the goal will be the same.
